## Sleep & Neuroplasticity Highlights
- Sleep consolidates learning; pulling all-nighters undermines retention.
- Eight tracked metrics drive the Neuroplasticity Sleep Score:

  5. WASO (minimize wake after sleep onset)
  6. Sleep Onset Latency (fall asleep within ~15 min)
  7. Respiratory Rate (stable, moderate rate)
  8. Deep Sleep %
